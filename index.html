FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A 36 hour event that brings participants together in teams of 1-4 to build any project that comes to their mind. There will be workshops, mentors, swag, food and entertainment and much more!

How many people are going to be there?

We’re expecting close to 450 students!

What if I’ve never coded before?

That’s totally cool, come and learn, beginners are certainly welcome.

What should I make?

Most participants from other hackathons like to make something related to software, hardware or art. Check out what others have been making at other hackathons at Devpost.com

Where is it?

At the University of Manitoba Multi Purpose Room in the University Centre (Main building)

When is it?

February 17th-19th, 2017

How much does it cost?

HackCentral is completely free! All you need to bring is yourself and a willingness to learn!

Will you be offering travel reimbursements?

We are currently not offering any travel reimbursements at this stage. If there is a possibility we will let you know.

Can I sleep?

You didn’t think we’d expect you to stay up for 36 hours straight right? Insanity!

Who can participate?

Any student in the world doing post-secondary education (Undergraduate and Graduate) are permitted to register.

What if I don’t have a team?

There will tons of people like you looking for a team, come make a friend!

What do I bring?

A laptop, cables, chargers, and your student card are crucial. If you’re making something hardware-related, be sure to bring the needed materials. A sleeping bag, pillow and toiletries are also quite useful.

Is food provided?

Of course! All the food to fuel your minds will be provided with dinner, lunch, snacks and drinks. Please let us know if you have any dietary restrictions in the application.

What are the rules?

You must not start coding your project until the event starts. Our event also abides by the MLH Code of Conduct linked above.

Have more questions?

Shoot us an email at info@hackcentral.ca
